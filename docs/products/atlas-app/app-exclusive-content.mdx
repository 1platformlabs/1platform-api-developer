# App-exclusive content

You can make certain titles available **only in your app**, giving your audience a reason to install and a way to reward mobile readers.

## What your audience gets

- **App-exclusive rail** — the editorial home includes a "Only in the app" rail that highlights titles your audience can't get on the web.
- **Exclusive badge** — any app-only title carries an "Exclusive in the app" badge wherever it appears, so readers immediately understand the value.
- **Clean web/app split** — the app only shows content meant for the mobile surface; web-only titles simply don't appear in the app, and app-only titles don't appear on the web.

## What you control

Each title in your catalog has an **availability** setting:

- **All** — visible everywhere (the default).
- **App-only** — visible only in the app, badged as exclusive.
- **Web-only** — visible only on the web, hidden in the app.

You set this per title in your tenant configuration. The app honors it automatically: it requests app-surface content and, as a safety check, still hides web-only items and badges app-only items even if they arrive from a cached payload.

## Why use it

App-exclusive content is a simple lever to grow installs and engagement — early access, bonus material, or app-first releases all become a reason for your audience to choose the app.
