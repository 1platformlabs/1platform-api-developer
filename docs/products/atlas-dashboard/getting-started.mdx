# Getting Started

This guide gets you from a fresh account to a live, branded storefront. No technical background is
required — everything is configured through the admin screens.

## 1. Sign in

Open your dashboard at your assigned address and sign in with the email invited by your platform
administrator. Your account is tied to a single tenant (your store) and to a role that controls what
you can see and change. See [Admin Roles](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/admin-roles) for what each
role can do.

## 2. Connect your own domain

Your storefront lives on **your own domain**. You add one DNS record at your domain registrar that
points your domain to the platform. Once the record propagates, every visitor who reaches your domain
sees your store — branding, copy, and configuration are matched to that host automatically.

| Step | What you do |
|---|---|
| Choose your domain | e.g. `store.yourbrand.com` or your apex domain |
| Add the DNS record | A simple record provided during onboarding |
| Wait for propagation | Usually minutes; can take longer |
| Verify | Visit the domain — your storefront should load |

You can also register additional host aliases so several domains resolve to the same store. See
[SEO Configuration](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/seo-configuration).

## 3. Set your brand basics

Before launch, set your logo, colors, and store name under
[Branding & Appearance](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/branding-appearance). The store reads these
from your tenant, so changes appear on the public site without a redeploy.

## 4. Add content and go live

Add your first items under [Content Management](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/content-management),
arrange the home page with [Promotional Rails](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/promotional-rails),
and review the [Overview](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/overview) for the full feature map.
