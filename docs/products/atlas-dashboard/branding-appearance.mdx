# Branding & Appearance

Branding lives in the **Settings** screen (`/admin/settings`) and controls every visible part of your
store's look. Because branding resolves per host, your changes appear on the public site automatically.

## Core branding

| Field | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Logo URL | Your store's logo |
| Favicon | The small icon in the browser tab (URL or upload) |
| OG image | The image used when your links are shared |
| Primary color | Your main brand color |
| Accent color | Secondary highlight color |
| Brand voice | The tone reflected across the store |
| Cover style | **Uploaded** (use your own cover art) or **Generated** (auto-create covers from title and author) |

## Appearance

Switch the store between **Light** and **Dark** mode to suit your brand. The default keeps existing
stores on their current look, so toggling is always a deliberate choice.

## Typeface voice

Choose between two typeface voices:

- **Cinematic** — a clean, modern sans-serif look suited to streaming-style stores.
- **Editorial** — a warm serif look suited to libraries and bookish stores.

Library-style stores default to the editorial voice; you can override this at any time.

## Advanced colors

For fine control, optional color fields let you set the background, surface, text, muted text, and
border colors individually. Leave them blank to fall back to the sensible defaults derived from your
primary and accent colors.
