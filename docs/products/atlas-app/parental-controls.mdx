# Parental controls

The Atlas App includes a parental-controls panel so families can keep content age-appropriate. The settings are protected by a PIN and stay on the device.

## What your audience gets

- **PIN gate** — the parental panel is locked behind a 4-digit PIN entered on a numeric keypad.
  - **First time** — the reader sets a PIN, which unlocks the panel.
  - **Returning** — the reader enters their PIN to get in; a wrong PIN flashes the dots and clears.
- **Restriction toggle** — a single switch turns restrictions on or off.
- **Maximum age rating** — chips let the parent pick the highest rating allowed (for example +7, +12, 16, +18), filtering what the app shows.
- **Change PIN** — the parent can update the parental PIN at any time.

The keypad keys are large, accessible touch targets, and the panel matches the rest of the account experience.

## What you control

Parental settings are **device-local** and self-service — each household configures their own restrictions and PIN, stored securely on their device. There is no per-tenant configuration required to offer this; it's available in any branded build.

This pairs naturally with the [biometric lock](/docs/products/atlas-app/profile-security) for households that want both an app-open lock and content restrictions.
