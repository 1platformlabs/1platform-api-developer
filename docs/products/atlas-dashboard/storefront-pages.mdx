# Storefront Pages

Your public storefront is generated automatically from your content and configuration. Each page is
branded with your tenant's logo, colors, copy, and typeface. This page maps the visitor-facing routes
so you know what your audience sees.

## Discovery

- **Home** — the landing page, arranged with your [Promotional Rails](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/promotional-rails).
- **Catalog** — the full browsable listing of your items.
- **Search** — visitors can search your catalog.
- **Genres** — genre index and per-genre pages.
- **Authors & publishers** — index and detail pages driven by your taxonomy.
- **People** — person pages (author, presenter, cast, etc.).

## Item & series pages

- **Item detail** — the page for a single book, video, podcast, or article. The main call to action
  ("Read", "Watch", or "Download") adapts to how you deliver content.
- **Show & episode pages** — for series with multiple episodes.

The URL path for items can be customized per kind (e.g. `/books/<slug>`) — see
[Redirects](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/redirects) for legacy URL handling.

## Live, app & commerce

- **Live** — live channel listing and individual channel pages (when live is enabled). See
  [Live Channels](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/live-channels).
- **Plans & purchase** — subscription and checkout pages. See
  [Subscription Tiers](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/subscription-tiers).
- **App page** — promotes your companion mobile app. See
  [App Storefront](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/app-storefront).

## Account & legal

Signed-in visitors get account pages including their saved library, registered devices, and
[Parental Controls](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/parental-controls). Privacy and terms pages are
also published as part of your store.

The text on these pages is fully editable — see
[Copy Customization](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/copy-customization).
