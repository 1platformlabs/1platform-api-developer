# Notifications

The **Notifications** screen (`/admin/notifications`) lets you reach your audience with announcements,
new releases, and reminders. You compose a message, choose how it's delivered and who receives it, and
review what you've sent.

## Composing a message

The composer sits beside a live preview so you can see your message as your audience will:

| Field | Notes |
|---|---|
| Title | The headline of the message |
| Body | The message text |
| Channel | **Push**, **Email**, or **In-app** |
| Audience | **All**, **Subscribers**, **Free users**, or **Inactive** |

When ready, you can **Send now**, **Schedule** for later, or **Save as draft**.

## Audiences

Targeting segments lets you tailor each message:

- **All** — everyone in your audience.
- **Subscribers** — paying members.
- **Free users** — members without a paid plan (a good segment for upgrade prompts).
- **Inactive** — members you'd like to win back.

## Delivery history

A history table records every message with its title, channel, audience, status (**Sent**,
**Scheduled**, or **Draft**), reach (how many were targeted), and the date and time. Use it to track
what went out and to revisit drafts and scheduled sends.
