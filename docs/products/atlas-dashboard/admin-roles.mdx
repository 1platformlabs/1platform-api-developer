# Admin Roles

The **Users / Members** screen (`/admin/users`) manages your dashboard team — who has access and what
they can do. This is separate from your audience; for subscribers and viewers see
[Members](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/members).

## Overview

Summary cards show your total members, how many are admins, and how many invitations are still pending.

## Inviting a teammate

Click **Invite** and enter the person's email, then choose their role. They receive an invitation and
appear in the table with an "Invited" status until they join.

## Roles

Each operator has one role that controls their permissions:

| Role | Typical access |
|---|---|
| Admin | Full access, including settings and team management |
| Operator | Day-to-day content and catalog work |
| Support | View-oriented access to help your audience |
| Reader | Read-only visibility |

A reference card grid on the screen describes each role so you can pick the right one.

## Changing a role

In the members table, change a person's role from the dropdown and save. Each member row also shows
their name, email, and when they were last active (or "Invited" if they haven't joined yet).
