# Getting started

The dashboard is your workspace control panel. This page covers signing in and getting oriented.

## Sign in with a magic link

There are no passwords. To sign in:

1. Open your workspace dashboard in the browser.
2. Enter the email address invited to the workspace.
3. Check your inbox for a one-time sign-in link.
4. Click the link — you land directly inside the dashboard.

The link is single-use and time-limited. If it expires, just request a new one. Because there is no password, there is nothing to store, reset, or leak.

## What you'll see first

After signing in you arrive at the **Dashboard** home, which greets you by name and shows your workspace at a glance: balance, usage this month, active domains, open invoices, and recent activity.

The left sidebar lists the modules enabled for your workspace. Common entries include Dashboard, Billing, Invoicing, and Domains, plus Settings and (for admins) Logs and Users. Which entries appear, and in what order, is configured per workspace — see [Modules](/docs/products/dashboard/modules).

## Finishing setup

New workspaces show an onboarding checklist that walks you through connecting a domain, generating your first content, setting up invoicing, and inviting your team. You can follow it step by step or skip it and return later. See [Onboarding](/docs/products/dashboard/onboarding).

## The top bar

Depending on your workspace configuration, the top bar may include a language switcher, a light/dark theme toggle, and a notifications bell. Your account menu lets you reach your profile and sign out.
