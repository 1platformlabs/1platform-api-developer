# Purchases & Rentals

The **Rentals / PPV** screen (`/admin/rentals`) tracks one-off purchases — pay-per-view events and
time-limited rentals — for stores that sell individual items rather than (or in addition to)
memberships.

This screen is available only when one-off purchases are enabled for your store. If it's turned off,
you'll see an explanation instead of the dashboard.

## At a glance

Four summary cards give you the headline numbers:

| Card | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Active rentals | Rentals currently within their viewing window |
| PPV revenue | Revenue from one-off purchases |
| Avg price | Average price across sales |
| Conversions | How many visitors completed a purchase |

## Sales table

The table lists each sellable item with:

- **Title** and its content kind badge.
- **Model** — **PPV** (pay-per-view) or **Rental**.
- **Price** — the amount charged.
- **Viewing window** — how long access lasts (for example, "Event" or "48 hours").
- **Sales** — how many times it has sold.
