# Analytics

The **Analytics** screen (`/admin/analytics`) shows how your audience behaves on your web storefront.
For your mobile app, see [App Analytics](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/app-analytics); for search
performance, see [SEO Health](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/seo-health).

## Headline metrics

Four summary cards each show a value and its trend versus the prior period:

| Card | Meaning |
|---|---|
| MAU | Monthly active users |
| VIP subscribers | Members on a premium tier |
| Active items | Items receiving engagement |
| Churn 30d | Members lost over 30 days |

## Top content

The **top items** section lists your best-performing content. Switch the range between **24h**, **7d**,
and **30d**, and review each item's title, slug, read count, and change versus the prior period — so you
can spot what's resonating.

## Activity trend

A daily activity chart shows engagement over recent days, giving you a quick read on momentum.

## Search performance

When your search console is connected, an additional card surfaces clicks, impressions, click-through
rate, average position, and your top queries directly in this view. If it isn't connected yet, you'll
see a prompt to connect it. Connect your search console under
[Tenant Settings](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/tenant-settings).

These dashboards are native to your store — no separate tool is required to read your audience trends.
