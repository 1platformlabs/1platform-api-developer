# Personalization

The Atlas App lets each reader shape the experience to their own taste. These preferences are stored on the device and apply instantly — they don't require any action from you.

## What your audience gets

- **Language** — readers choose the interface language from your tenant's enabled languages, or "Automatic" to follow the device. The app re-translates right away and remembers the choice across restarts.
- **Appearance** — Light, Dark, or "Automatic" to follow your brand theme. The app re-themes immediately, with no flash on the next launch because the choice is applied during startup.
- **Reading preferences** — in the reader, each person sets their own theme, typeface (Serif/Sans), font size, line height, margins, brightness, and paged or scrolling flow. These stay on the device and never sync.
- **Notification preferences** — readers pick which kinds of notifications they want (see [Push notifications](/docs/products/atlas-app/push-notifications)).

## What you control

- **Default theme** — your brand theme is the starting point; readers can override Light/Dark locally.
- **Available languages** — the languages a reader can choose come from your tenant configuration.

Everything here is a per-reader, device-local choice layered on top of your brand defaults, so your audience gets a comfortable, personal experience while your branding remains the foundation.
