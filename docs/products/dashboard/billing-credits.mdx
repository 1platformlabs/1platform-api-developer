# Billing & credits

The **Credits & billing** page is where you manage your balance and review what you've been charged. Everything is pay-as-you-go: you top up credits and they're drawn down as you use the workspace.

## Your balance

A balance card at the top shows your current credit balance and your plan label. The big **Top up credits** button opens the top-up dialog.

## Top up credits

1. Select **Top up credits** (from the balance card, the page header, or a quick action elsewhere in the dashboard).
2. Choose or enter the amount you want to add.
3. Confirm. Your balance and history refresh automatically once the top-up completes.

You can also reach this dialog directly from the **Top Up Credits** quick action on the dashboard home.

## Recent transactions

Below the balance, the **Recent transactions** section lists your activity. Each row shows a description, date, type, amount, and status:

| Type | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Top-up** | Credits you added to your balance. |
| **Subscription** | A recurring charge. |
| **Invoice / usage** | Credits consumed by workspace activity. |
| **Refund** | Credits returned to you. |

While transactions load you'll see placeholder rows. If you have none yet, an empty state explains that charges, top-ups, and usage will appear here as you start billing.
