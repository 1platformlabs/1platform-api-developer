# Settings — Notifications

The **Notifications** tab lives under Settings, between Billing and API.

## Coming soon

This pane is a **planned feature**. When it ships, it will let you fine-tune how and when the workspace contacts you, including:

- **Per-channel preferences** — choose which channels notify you.
- **Per-category preferences** — decide which kinds of events you want to hear about.

Until then, the tab displays a friendly "coming soon" message so the planned scope is clear.

## Notifications today

Depending on your workspace configuration, a **notifications bell** may appear in the top bar, giving you quick access to recent alerts. Whether the bell is shown is part of the per-workspace layout configuration — see [Branding & appearance](/docs/products/dashboard/branding-appearance) and [Modules](/docs/products/dashboard/modules).

When the Notifications tab arrives, these top-bar alerts and your channel and category choices will be configurable from one place. If you need a notification preference changed before then, contact your workspace administrator.
