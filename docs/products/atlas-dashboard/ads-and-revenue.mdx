# Ads & Revenue

The **Ads Manager** (`/admin/ads`) lets you manage advertising placements on your storefront and
review how they perform. It is one of several ways to monetize your store, alongside
[Subscription Tiers](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/subscription-tiers) and
[Purchases & Rentals](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/purchases-and-rentals).

## Performance at a glance

Four summary cards show your 30-day advertising performance:

| Card | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Impressions | How many times ads were shown |
| Fill rate | Share of ad requests that were filled |
| eCPM | Effective earnings per thousand impressions |
| Revenue | Ad revenue over the period |

## Placements

The table lists each ad **slot** by name and key, its format, impressions, eCPM, and an
enable/disable toggle (shown read-only if you don't have edit rights). Use the toggles to turn
individual placements on or off without removing them.

## Configuration

The connection details for your advertising are set under
[Tenant Settings](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/tenant-settings) — the Ads Manager links you there
when configuration is needed. As with all integrations, these are presented as native capabilities of
your store.
