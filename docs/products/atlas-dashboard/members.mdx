# Members

Atlas separates two kinds of "people": your **audience** (subscribers and viewers) and your
**team** (operators with dashboard access). This page covers the audience and content people; for
who can administer the store, see [Admin Roles](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/admin-roles).

## Your audience

Your subscribers and their status appear in
[Subscription Tiers](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/subscription-tiers) under the Subscriptions
dashboard — email, plan, status, and revenue contribution per member. Broader audience trends (active
members, conversions, churn) live in [Analytics](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/analytics).

You can also reach your audience directly through
[Notifications](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/notifications), targeting segments such as all members,
subscribers, free users, or inactive members.

## Cast & crew

The **Cast & Crew** screen (`/admin/people`) is a directory of the people credited in your content —
authors, directors, presenters, and similar roles depending on your store type. Search by name to find
a person; each result card shows their avatar, name, role, and how many items credit them. These pages
also power the public person pages on your storefront.

## Account self-service

Signed-in audience members manage their own accounts on the storefront, including saved library,
[registered devices](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/parental-controls), and parental controls — you
don't manage these per-member from the admin panel.
