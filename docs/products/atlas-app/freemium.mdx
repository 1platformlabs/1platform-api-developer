# Freemium

The Atlas App supports a freemium model: a free experience for everyone, with member benefits unlocked by a paid plan that is purchased and managed on the web.

## What your audience gets

- **Free access** — by default a reader signs in and goes straight into the app at no cost.
- **Paywall (optional)** — if you require membership, a reader who isn't a paid member sees a branded paywall after sign-in. It shows an eyebrow ("Your access"), a headline, a plan card with a price and a feature checklist, a primary call-to-action, and a "continue for free" option where allowed.
- **Web checkout** — the paywall's call-to-action opens your tenant's website to subscribe; the app never uses native in-app purchases. When membership is confirmed, the reader is moved into the app automatically.
- **Free-tier limits** — free readers get a capped number of downloads per day; members get unlimited. The free tier may also include ads (presented as a native feature).

## What you control

Two per-tenant settings shape your model:

- **Subscription required** — when on, non-members are routed to the paywall after sign-in. When off (the default for free libraries), everyone goes straight in.
- **Plans & limits** — your plan catalog, prices and the free-tier download cap are configured for your tenant and managed on the web.

This lets the same app be fully free for one tenant and gated for another, with no code changes.
