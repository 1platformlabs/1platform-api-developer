# Invoicing

The **Invoicing** page lets you issue and track electronic invoices for your workspace.

## The summary cards

Four cards give you the headline figures for the period you're viewing:

| Card | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Issued (MTD)** | Invoices issued month-to-date, with the change vs. last month. |
| **Total billed** | The combined value of invoices in the selected window. |
| **Outstanding** | Value of invoices sent but not yet settled. |
| **Overdue** | Count of invoices past their due date. |

## Finding invoices

The invoice table can be narrowed in three ways:

1. **Status tabs** — All, Draft, Sent, Paid, Overdue, Cancelled.
2. **Search** — by invoice number, customer, or amount.
3. **Time range** — last 30 days, last 90 days, this month, or this year.

A footer shows how many invoices are visible and the active date window.

## Issuing a new invoice

Select **New invoice** to begin. You first choose the issuing business, so make sure at least one is configured — see [Invoicing businesses](/docs/products/dashboard/invoicing-businesses). If you have none yet, the empty state links you straight to the Businesses screen to set up your first issuer.

## Exporting

Use **Export CSV** to download the invoices currently shown (matching your filters and search) as a spreadsheet file. The export respects whatever tabs, search text, and time range are active, so you get exactly the slice you're looking at.

## Statuses you'll see

Invoices move through *Draft*, *Sent*, and may end as *Cancelled*. The status tab you choose filters the table to just those invoices.
