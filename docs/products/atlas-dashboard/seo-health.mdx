# SEO Health

The **SEO Health** screen (`/admin/seo-health`) tracks how your storefront performs in search and lets
you request indexing for specific URLs. It pairs with the on-page settings in
[SEO Configuration](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/seo-configuration).

## Performance summary

Four cards show your search performance, each with a week-over-week change:

| Card | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Clicks | Visits from search results |
| Impressions | Times your pages appeared in search |
| CTR | Click-through rate |
| Position | Average ranking position |

## Sitemap coverage

A coverage line shows how many of your URLs are indexed (e.g. "X of Y URLs indexed"), and a daily
clicks chart shows the recent trend.

## Top queries

A table lists your top search queries with clicks, impressions, CTR, average position, and the
week-over-week click change — so you can see which searches bring people to your store.

## Requesting indexing

To prompt a fresh crawl, paste one URL per line, choose the operation, and submit:

- **Re-crawl** — tell search engines a page was updated.
- **Remove** — tell search engines a page was deleted.

## Alerts

The screen warns you automatically when something needs attention — for example a sharp week-over-week
drop in clicks, or sitemap coverage falling below a healthy threshold.

Connect your search console under
[Tenant Settings](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/tenant-settings) so this data flows in.
