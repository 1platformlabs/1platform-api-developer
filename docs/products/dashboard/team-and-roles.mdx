# Team & roles

Managing your team has two surfaces: the admin **Users** screen (available now) and the **Team** tab under Settings (planned).

## Managing users (admins)

The admin **Users** screen lets workspace administrators control who can sign in. From here you can:

- **Invite a user** — select **Invite user** and send an invitation by email.
- **Search** — find people by email or username.
- **Review status** — each member shows whether they're *Active* or *Inactive*, and whether their email is verified.
- **View as** — administrators can step into a member's view to troubleshoot (see [Admin & impersonation](/docs/products/dashboard/admin-impersonation)).

The list is paginated, so use the **Previous** and **Next** controls to move between pages when you have many members. If there are no members yet, an empty state invites you to bring your team on board.

## The Team tab (coming soon)

The **Team** tab under Settings is a **planned feature**. When it ships it will consolidate team management for everyone, including:

- **Members** — see who's in the workspace.
- **Invites** — send and track invitations.
- **Roles** — assign roles to members.
- **Access revocation** — remove access when someone leaves.

Until then, the tab shows a "coming soon" message, and administrators use the Users screen above.

## Sign-in reminder

Everyone signs in with a **magic link** — invited members receive a one-time link by email, so there are no passwords to distribute. See [Getting started](/docs/products/dashboard/getting-started).
