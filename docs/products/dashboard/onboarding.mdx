# Onboarding

New workspaces open with a guided **setup checklist**. Most teams finish in under ten minutes, and you can skip it at any time and return later.

## The free-credits banner

At the top, a banner reminds you that a new workspace comes with free credits to explore — enough to try generating content, issuing an invoice, or testing an integration. No card is required to get started.

## The checklist

The wizard walks you through setup in order. A progress bar shows how far along you are.

| Step | What you do |
|---|---|
| **Create your account** | Done as soon as your workspace is ready. |
| **Verify your email** | Confirms the address tied to your account. |
| **Connect your first domain** | Point a domain so you can start publishing. |
| **Generate your first article** | Try the content pipeline end to end. |
| **Issue your first invoice** | Set up your tax and issuer details. |
| **Invite your team** | Add editors, viewers, and admins. |

Each open step has a button that takes you straight to the right screen. Completed steps are checked off and counted toward the progress bar.

## Helpful resources

Below the checklist, resource cards link you to documentation, a product tour, and a way to contact support — so help is one click away while you set things up.

## Skipping and returning

Select **Skip for now** to go straight to the [Dashboard home](/docs/products/dashboard/dashboard-home). The checklist remains available so you can finish setup whenever you're ready.
