# App Analytics

The **App Analytics** screen (`/admin/app-analytics`) shows how people use your companion mobile app.
It complements the web-focused [Analytics](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/analytics) screen and is
populated once measurement is enabled under
[App Storefront](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/app-storefront).

## Choosing a range

Range buttons let you switch the whole view between the last **24h**, **7d**, or **30d**.

## Headline metrics

Five summary cards each show a number and a small trend line:

| Card | Meaning |
|---|---|
| DAU | Daily active users |
| MAU | Monthly active users |
| Reads | Content opened/read in the app |
| Downloads | Items downloaded for offline use |
| Conversions | Members who converted (e.g. to a paid plan) |

## Conversion funnel

The **funnel** section visualizes a multi-step path through your app — each step shows its name, count,
and percentage of the top step — so you can see where users drop off on the way to converting.

## Event series

The **event series** picker lets you chart a single event over time. Choose an event (such as app
open, update prompt, content open, content finish, download, paywall view, or paywall CTA) and apply
it; a table then lists the date and count for that event across the selected range.

All of this is presented as a native part of your store — connect your measurement in
[App Storefront](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/app-storefront) to start collecting data.
