# SEO Configuration

SEO settings live under [Tenant Settings](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/tenant-settings)
(`/admin/settings`). They control how your storefront appears in search results and when shared on
social media. Everything is editable per tenant and resolves per host.

## SEO blocks (on-page features)

A grid of checkboxes turns optional on-page SEO blocks on or off, grouped by area:

- **Home blocks** — meta tags, hero item counter, headline, long copy.
- **Item detail** — metadata panel, related-by-author, structured data for items.
- **Taxonomy & catalog** — genre cloud and related groupings.
- **Footer** and **Schema.org** structured-data blocks.

Each block renders only when enabled, so you control exactly what appears.

## SEO copy & templates

The SEO copy section sets the text and templates used across your store:

| Field | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Canonical host | Your preferred domain for canonical URLs |
| OG locale / site name | Social-sharing language and brand name |
| Title & description templates | Patterns for home, item, taxonomy, and catalog titles |
| Home headline & long copy | Editorial text on the home page |
| Format FAQ / legal note | Optional structured FAQ and legal text |

## Social cards

Choose the social card style (summary or large image) and set your OG image so shared links look
polished.

## Search-engine verification

Paste the verification codes provided by each search or social console (Google, Bing, Yandex,
Pinterest, Facebook). The platform inserts them correctly into your pages so you can verify ownership.

## Host aliases & robots

You can register additional **host aliases** so multiple domains resolve to your store, and set robots
and crawler directives. To monitor results, see [SEO Health](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/seo-health).
For URL changes, see [Redirects](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/redirects).
