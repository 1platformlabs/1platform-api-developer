# Tenant Settings

The **Settings** screen (`/admin/settings`) is the central place to configure your store. It gathers
several areas into one page, each in its own section. Most settings resolve per host, so changes appear
on your public storefront automatically.

## Sections

| Section | What it covers | Detailed guide |
|---|---|---|
| Branding | Logo, favicon, OG image, colors, brand voice, cover style | [Branding & Appearance](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/branding-appearance) |
| Appearance & typeface | Light/dark mode, cinematic/editorial voice, advanced colors | [Branding & Appearance](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/branding-appearance) |
| Integrations | Connect your advertising, measurement, and search console | [Ads & Revenue](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/ads-and-revenue), [Analytics](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/analytics) |
| Custom code | Paste head/body snippets into the public site | [Custom Code](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/custom-code) |
| SEO blocks & copy | On-page SEO features, titles, descriptions, templates | [SEO Configuration](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/seo-configuration) |
| Verification | Search and social ownership codes | [SEO Configuration](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/seo-configuration) |
| Footer | Social links and footer background | (this page) |

## Footer

The **Footer** section configures the links and look of your storefront footer: social links (such as
X, Telegram, RSS, mail, LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Kick, Facebook, and your website) and the
footer's background color. Only the links you fill in are shown.

## Integrations

All integrations are presented as native capabilities of your store. You connect advertising,
measurement, and search performance here, and the matching dashboards (Ads, Analytics, SEO Health)
light up once connected. No external brand names appear on your storefront.

## Saving

Use the **Save** action to apply changes. The screen also provides a **Reset** action to discard
unsaved edits.
