# Parental Controls

Parental controls and device management are **self-service** features your audience uses on the public
storefront — they are not edited per-member from the admin panel. This page explains what your visitors
see so you can support them.

## Parental controls

Signed-in members can set parental controls on their account from the storefront's account area. These
let a member restrict the content their household can access, so younger viewers only see appropriate
items in your catalog.

Because controls live on the member's account, each member manages their own household settings. As an
operator, your part is to classify your content correctly — the **content rating** for your mobile app
is set under [App Storefront](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/app-storefront), and item-level
availability is set on each item in
[Content Management](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/content-management).

## Registered devices

Members can also view and manage their **registered devices** from their account. This shows the
devices signed in to their account so they can review and remove any they no longer use — useful for
account security and for households sharing a plan.

## Where these pages live

These pages are part of the account section of your storefront. For the full list of public pages, see
[Storefront Pages](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/storefront-pages). The wording on these pages can be
adjusted in [Copy Customization](/docs/products/atlas-dashboard/copy-customization).
